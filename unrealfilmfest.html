Submission Categories

We currently have three categories accepted
Under 5 mins

Mini films or music videos that are up to 4 mins and 59 seconds will be considered in this category. The fee for this category is ₹500 (one time fee, non refundable. Selection and screening is dependent on the judgment panel's decision.)

Up to 10 mins

Short films that are up to 9 mins and 59 seconds will be considered in this category. The fee for this category is ₹700 (one time fee, non refundable. Selection and screening is dependent on the judgment panel's decision.)

Up to 30 mins

Films that are up to 29 mins and 59 seconds will be considered in this category. The fee for this category is ₹1000 (one time fee, non refundable. Selection and screening is dependent on the judgment panel's decision.)

Steps

The below steps will be followed for the entire event from submission to closing.

1

Submission

The participant needs to upload and complete payment to register in the film festival
2

Selection

Once the submission window closes, the judgement panel will review the submissions. Based on the selection status an email will be sent out to all the participants.
3

Event

The screening and award distribution event will be held in Dumdum Cantonment, Kolkata, India. The exact date will be updated in the website after closure of the submission window.

Frequently asked questions

  • Can film of any language be submitted?
    Yes, however for films other that Bengali language must have subtitles.
  • Is there any specific subject/theme?
    There are no specific themes and films from any genre can be submitted.
  • Is the screening guaranteed for all the submissions?
    To keep the quality of the festival up to the mark, we may not select all the films for screening. But as Unreal Film Festival is mainly to highlight the uprising indie filmmakers, we have not kept our bars on stratospheric level.
  • Will I get refund if my movie is not selected?
    The fees are non-returnable. However in case of some emergency where the festival needs to be cancelled the fees will be refunded regardless of selection status.
  • What will be the location of the film festival?
    The location of the festival will be Dum Dum Cantonment, Nalta Recreation Club. Detailed location is given in the map below. Date will be announced in this website also will be notified over email to all participants once submission window is closed.
